Medical Career Guide

General Medicine (MBBS)

Graduation:

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)

Post-Graduation:

  • MD (General Medicine, Pediatrics, etc.)
  • MS (General Surgery, ENT, etc.)
  • DM/MCh (Super-Specialty)

Job Titles:

  • General Physician
  • Surgeon
  • Pediatrician
  • Specialist Doctor
  • Hospital Administrator

Dentistry

Graduation:

  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

Post-Graduation:

  • MDS (Orthodontics, Oral Surgery, etc.)

Job Titles:

  • Dentist
  • Oral Surgeon
  • Orthodontist
  • Dental Consultant

Pharmacy

Graduation:

  • B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy)

Post-Graduation:

  • M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacology, etc.)
  • Pharm.D (Doctor of Pharmacy)

Job Titles:

  • Pharmacist
  • Drug Inspector
  • Clinical Research Associate
  • Pharma Marketing Executive

Nursing

Graduation:

  • B.Sc. Nursing
  • GNM (General Nursing and Midwifery)

Post-Graduation:

  • M.Sc. Nursing (Various Specializations)

Job Titles:

  • Registered Nurse
  • Nurse Practitioner
  • Head Nurse
  • Clinical Nurse Specialist

Physiotherapy

Graduation:

  • BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

Post-Graduation:

  • MPT (Various Specializations like Ortho, Neuro, Sports)

Job Titles:

  • Physiotherapist
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Sports Therapist

Paramedical & Allied Health Sciences

Branches Include:

  • Medical Lab Technology (MLT)
  • Radiology
  • Operation Theatre Technology
  • Anesthesia Technology
  • Dialysis Technology

Graduation:

  • B.Sc. in relevant field
  • Diploma in Paramedical Courses

Job Titles:

  • Lab Technician
  • Radiology Technician
  • OT Technician
  • Dialysis Technician
