Law Career Guide

Core Law Education

Graduation:

  • LL.B. (3-year after graduation)
  • B.A. LL.B., B.Com. LL.B., B.B.A. LL.B. (5-year integrated)

Post-Graduation:

  • LL.M. (Master of Law in various specializations)
  • Ph.D. in Law

Job Titles:

  • Advocate
  • Legal Advisor
  • Corporate Lawyer
  • Judge (via Judicial Services Exam)
  • Legal Analyst
  • Law Professor

Specialized Law Fields

Specializations:

  • Criminal Law
  • Civil Law
  • Corporate Law
  • Constitutional Law
  • Intellectual Property Law
  • Cyber Law
  • Taxation Law
  • Environmental Law
  • International Law
  • Labor and Employment Law
  • Family Law

Post-Graduation Options:

  • LL.M. in Criminal Law, Business Law, Constitutional Law, etc.
  • Diploma/Certificate in specialized areas (Cyber Law, IPR, etc.)

Job Titles:

  • Criminal Lawyer
  • IPR Specialist
  • Cyber Law Expert
  • Constitutional Law Expert
  • Arbitration & Mediation Specialist

Legal Services & Government Sector

Career Options:

  • Judiciary Services (Judge, Magistrate)
  • Public Prosecutor
  • Legal Officer (PSUs, Govt. Depts)
  • Law Officer in Ministries
  • Notary Public
  • Legal Consultant for NGOs

Exams & Requirements:

  • Judicial Services Exam (PCS-J)
  • Bar Council Exam (AIBE)
  • UPSC (for legal service posts)

Corporate & Business Law

Fields:

  • Corporate Law
  • Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Compliance and Regulation
  • Contract Law
  • Banking & Finance Law

Job Titles:

  • In-House Legal Counsel
  • Compliance Officer
  • Legal Risk Analyst
  • Contract Manager

Industries:

  • Multinational Companies
  • Startups
  • Banks & Financial Institutions
  • Law Firms
