Alternative Career Paths Guide

Creative and Design Fields

Graduation Courses:

  • Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA)
  • Bachelor of Design (B.Des)
  • Bachelor in Fashion Design
  • Bachelor in Animation or Multimedia

Post-Graduation Options:

  • MFA (Master of Fine Arts)
  • M.Des (Master of Design)
  • PG in Animation & VFX
  • MBA in Fashion Management

Job Titles:

  • Graphic Designer
  • UI/UX Designer
  • Animator
  • Fashion Designer
  • Art Director
  • Creative Director

Media and Communication

Graduation Courses:

  • Bachelor in Mass Communication
  • Bachelor in Journalism
  • Bachelor in Media Studies

Post-Graduation Options:

  • Master in Journalism and Mass Comm
  • PG Diploma in Film/TV Direction

Job Titles:

  • News Anchor
  • Reporter
  • Content Creator
  • Copywriter
  • Video Editor

Commerce and Management

Graduation Courses:

  • B.Com (General, Honors)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • CA, CMA, CS (Professional Courses)

Post-Graduation Options:

  • M.Com
  • MBA (Various Specializations)
  • PGDM

Job Titles:

  • Accountant
  • Auditor
  • Business Analyst
  • Marketing Manager
  • HR Executive

Hospitality and Tourism

Post-Graduation Options:

Job Titles:

Education and Social Work
