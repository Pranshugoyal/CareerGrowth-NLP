Government Services Career Guide

Branches of Government Services

  • Administrative Services (IAS, PCS)
  • Police Services (IPS, State Police)
  • Revenue Services (IRS, State Revenue Dept)
  • Foreign Services (IFS)
  • Education Services
  • Engineering Services (IES, PWD, CPWD)
  • Judicial Services
  • Defense Civil Services
  • Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs)
  • Municipal and Local Governance

Graduation Level Entry

Degrees Required:

  • Bachelor’s in any discipline (B.A., B.Com., B.Sc., B.E., etc.)
  • Bachelor of Law (LL.B.) for Judicial & Legal roles
  • B.Ed. for Government Teaching roles

Major Entry Exams:

  • UPSC Civil Services Examination (IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS)
  • SSC CGL, CHSL, JE (for central govt roles)
  • State Public Service Commission Exams (PCS)
  • RRB NTPC/Group D (Railways)
  • Bank Exams (IBPS, SBI PO/Clerk, RBI Grade B)
  • PSU Exams (via GATE)

Post-Graduation Options

  • M.A. in Public Administration, Political Science, Economics
  • MBA for PSU management roles
  • LL.M. for higher judiciary roles
  • M.Tech (for Engineering Services via GATE)
  • B.Ed. + M.Ed. for School Principal/Officer roles

Job Titles in Government Services

Administrative Services:

  • Indian Administrative Service (IAS) Officer
  • State Civil Services Officer (SDM, Tehsildar)
  • Block Development Officer
  • Municipal Commissioner

Police & Security Services:

  • Indian Police Service (IPS) Officer
  • State Police Services (DSP, Inspector)
  • Intelligence Bureau Officer
  • Narcotics & Cybercrime Officer

Revenue Services:

  • Indian Revenue Service (IRS)
  • Income Tax Officer
  • GST/Customs Officer

Foreign & Diplomatic Services:

  • Indian Foreign Service (IFS)
  • Embassy Attaché
  • Consulate Officer

Other Government Jobs:

  • SSC Officer (Audit, Tax, Statistical, Clerk)
  • Railway Officer (RRB)
  • Bank Officer (IBPS/SBI PO)
  • Teacher/Lecturer (State/UGC NET)
  • PSU Officer (GAIL, ONGC, BHEL, etc.)

Additional Roles & Resources

  • Public Prosecutor (via State Law Service)
  • Election Officer/Returning Officer
  • Probation Officer / Social Welfare Officer
  • RTI & Vigilance Officers
  • Parliamentary/Legislative Assistant
  • Training: LBSNAA, NADT, IGNFA, SVPNPA
