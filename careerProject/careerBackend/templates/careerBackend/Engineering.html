Engineering Career Guide

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Graduation:

  • B.E. / B.Tech in CSE

Post-Graduation:

  • M.E. / M.Tech in CSE
  • MBA in IT
  • M.S. in Computer Science

Job Titles:

  • Software Developer
  • Web Developer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Analyst

Mechanical Engineering

Graduation:

  • B.E. / B.Tech in Mechanical

Post-Graduation:

  • M.E. / M.Tech in Thermal, Design, Manufacturing, etc.
  • MBA in Operations

Job Titles:

  • Mechanical Design Engineer
  • Project Engineer
  • Automobile Engineer
  • HVAC Engineer

Electrical Engineering

Graduation:

  • B.E. / B.Tech in Electrical Engineering

Post-Graduation:

  • M.E. / M.Tech in Power Systems, Control Systems, etc.
  • MBA in Energy Management

Job Titles:

  • Electrical Design Engineer
  • Power System Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Instrumentation Engineer

Civil Engineering

Graduation:

  • B.E. / B.Tech in Civil

Post-Graduation:

  • M.E. / M.Tech in Structural, Transportation, Environmental, etc.
  • MBA in Construction Management

Job Titles:

  • Site Engineer
  • Structural Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • Construction Planner

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Graduation:

  • B.E. / B.Tech in ECE

Post-Graduation:

  • M.E. / M.Tech in VLSI, Embedded Systems, Communication
  • MBA in Telecom

Job Titles:

  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • VLSI Design Engineer
  • Telecom Engineer
  • Electronics Design Engineer
