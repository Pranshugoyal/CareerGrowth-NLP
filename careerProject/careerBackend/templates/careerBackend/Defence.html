Defense Career Guide

Branches of Indian Defense Forces

  • Indian Army
  • Indian Navy
  • Indian Air Force
  • Indian Coast Guard
  • Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF)
  • Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO)
  • National Security Guard (NSG)

Graduation Level Entry

Courses:

  • National Defence Academy (NDA) - B.Sc. (Defence Studies)
  • Indian Military Academy (IMA) - Graduate Entry
  • Naval Academy (10+2 B.Tech Entry)
  • Air Force Academy - B.Sc. (Flying Branch, Ground Duties)
  • Bachelorâ€™s in Engineering for Technical Entry Scheme (TES)

Entry Exams:

  • NDA & NA Exam (UPSC)
  • CDS (Combined Defence Services Exam - UPSC)
  • AFCAT (Air Force Common Admission Test)
  • INET (Indian Navy Entrance Test)

Post-Graduation Options

  • Master's in Defence and Strategic Studies
  • M.Tech from DRDO/AFMC-related defense tech institutions
  • PG Diploma in Disaster Management, Military Psychology
  • Ph.D. in Strategic Affairs, Military History, National Security

Job Titles in Defense Sector

Commissioned Officers:

  • Lieutenant / Sub-Lieutenant / Flying Officer
  • Captain / Lieutenant / Flight Lieutenant
  • Major / Lt. Commander / Squadron Leader
  • Colonel / Commander / Wing Commander
  • Brigadier / Commodore / Group Captain
  • Major General / Rear Admiral / Air Vice Marshal
  • Lieutenant General / Vice Admiral / Air Marshal

Non-Commissioned & Technical Roles:

  • Technical Officer
  • Radar/Navigational Officer
  • Armament Officer
  • Signals and Communication Specialist

Defense Civilian Roles:

  • Scientist (DRDO)
  • Defense Analyst
  • Defense Journalist
  • Procurement & Logistics Officer

Specialized Defense Careers

  • Judge Advocate General (JAG) - Legal Wing of the Armed Forces
  • Medical Officer (via Armed Forces Medical Services)
  • Intelligence Officer (via RAW, DIA, IB)
  • Cyber Defense Specialist
  • Space and Missile Defense Officer
  • Combat Engineer
  • Pilot/Navigator (Air Force and Navy)
